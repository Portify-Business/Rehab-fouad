Rehab Fouad

Assistant manager at Baheya's Boutique

  • Welcome to my website! I am currently honored to serve as an assistant manager at Baheya Cancer Institute, where I play a crucial role in Baheya's boutique, providing invaluable psychological support to cancer patients.
  • My professional journey has been a deeply fulfilling one, characterized by a commitment to human development. It all began when I started as a field training officer at Afaq, where I honed my skills in managing human resources. This early role laid a strong foundation for my future endeavors, including a rewarding stint as the training manager for the Egyptian-American youth rehabilitation project, where I had the privilege of empowering young minds.
  • Transitioning to the role of administration manager at Creative Academy, I showcased my organizational prowess and leadership abilities. My time as a human development trainer at Misr Public Library further fueled my passion for imparting knowledge and skills to others. Additionally, my involvement in evaluating committees for private schools underscored my dedication to education.
  • I hold certifications in Training of Trainer, Human Resources, and Communication Skills from the American University of Science. I also have training in psychological counseling and political awareness. Additionally, I volunteer at Baheya Hospital and the Hope Giver Campaign.
  • Alongside my role at Baheya Cancer Institute, I am deeply passionate about crochet and run a small business in this craft. Crochet is more than just a hobby for me; it's a way to express my creativity and create beautiful, handmade items. Each stitch is a labor of love, and I take great joy in sharing my creations with others. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work !
